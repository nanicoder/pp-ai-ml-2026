The Best Movies According to Pradeep

My top 3 movies of all time.

1. The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

This movie tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover, despite his claims of innocence.

2. The Godfather (1972)

This movie tells the story of the powerful Italian-American crime family of the Corleone family, headed by Vito Corleone.

3. The Dark Knight (2008)

This movie tells the story of Batman's battle against the Joker, who is trying to create chaos in Gotham City.
